## Who wins what

- **SlabClaw wins technical depth** if the judge rewards complex on-chain/agentic infrastructure above all else.
- **SOLVENT wins generic business-loop completeness** if the judge wants a broad autonomous-company demo rather than a specialized quant thesis.
- **StackFund is the closest thesis rival** but is weaker if it cannot show rejected strategies and frozen evidence.
- **AQTC wins scientific integrity/provenance** because it shows both the alpha that passed and the alpha that failed.

## AQTC's one-sentence differentiator

Most competitors show an agent doing work. AQTC shows an agent operating on evidence that can be falsified.

## What not to overclaim

- Do not claim live trading.
- Do not claim live Stripe/NVIDIA proof unless the artifact was generated with real test credentials.
- Do not claim AQTC is universally more complete than SOLVENT or technically deeper than SlabClaw.
- Do claim AQTC is more evidence-grounded than prompt-trading demos.
