# AQTC Hackathon Demo — Voice Script (1:35 target, 6 scenes)

Voiceover dry, calm, deliberate. No music. No hype. Engineering voice.

---

## Scene 1 — HOOK (0:00–0:14)

**Visual:** black screen → AQTC logo / project title card
**Voice:**
> "Most AI trading demos are exactly three things: a prompt, a signal, and a chart. They hide bad backtests, they skip business operations, and they cannot explain where the alpha came from."

---

## Scene 1b — WHAT IS FINANCIAL LAB (0:14–0:30)

**Visual:** infographic — "Two repositories, one pipeline" (Financial Lab vs AQTC)
**Voice:**
> "Quick clarification: Financial Lab is a separate, open source repository. It is the research engine that evolved this alpha with evolution strategies. AQTC, this repository, takes that frozen evidence and operates it as a business."

Added because the scene 1 visual names "Financial Lab" before any narration
explains what it is — judges watching cold were left wondering whether it's
part of this codebase. This scene answers that immediately, before scene 2
goes into HGAT+ES technical detail.

## Scene 2 — THE DIFFERENCE (0:30–0:50)

**Visual:** terminal recording — `aqtc provenance`
**Voice:**
> "Autonomous Quant Company does it differently. First, we prove the alpha origin. Frozen Financial Lab evidence: a heterogeneous graph attention network optimized with evolution strategies. Mean Sharpe three point two six, five folds, one hundred percent positive, max drawdown zero point zero three."

---

## Scene 3 — FALSIFICATION (0:50–1:05)

**Visual:** terminal recording — rejected candidate card / dashboard card
**Voice:**
> "Then we falsify bad strategies explicitly. A twenty-nineteen ensemble is not buried, it is rejected with full evidence: Sharpe minus zero point five four, max drawdown forty-eight percent. The system refuses to hide it."

---

## Scene 4 — BUSINESS LOOP (1:05–1:28)

**Visual:** terminal recording — `aqtc demo --json`, dashboard :8010
**Voice:**
> "Then the business loop runs. A policy boundary before any live action. Nemotron summarises the regime. The Stripe-style ledger records a two dollar data purchase, then a nineteen dollar customer report. Net operating result: seventeen dollars. Paper broker holds four positions, gross exposure capped at four."

---

## Scene 5 — CLOSE (1:28–1:35)

**Visual:** dashboard overview → README headline
**Voice:**
> "From evolved alpha to invoice. No prompt trading, no theatre. Hermes agent operations, Stripe revenue. Open source."

## Tone notes

- Pace: roughly 150 wpm. Slight pauses at colons and numbers.
- Engineer voice, not salesperson. No superlatives ("amazing", "revolutionary").
- Numbers spoken naturally ("three point two six" not "three-point-two-five-five").
- Acronyms expanded: "HGAT+ES" → "heterogeneous graph attention network optimized with evolution strategies" (Scene 2). "NemoClaw" simplified to "policy boundary".
- Final sentence is the tagline. No prize mention (cut from original).
