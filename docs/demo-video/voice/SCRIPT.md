## Tone notes

- Pace: roughly 150 wpm. Slight pauses at colons and numbers.
- Engineer voice, not salesperson. No superlatives ("amazing", "revolutionary").
- Numbers spoken digit by digit where helpful ("three point two five five" not "three-point-two-five-five").
- The final sentence states the prize; do not oversell.
